Mahalingam Ramkumar is an Associate Professor of Computer Science at Mississippi State University. He received his BS from the University of Madras, India,   M.S. from Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, India  and Ph.D from New Jersey Institute of Technology, Newark, NJ. His research interests include trustworthy computing, security models, applied cryptography, and network/system security.
